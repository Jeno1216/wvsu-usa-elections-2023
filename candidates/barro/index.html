Platform

CAScouts

Shaping future Taga-CAS

This advocacy was formulated due constant participation of the proponent to the extensions Committee of the ASSC Inc. This aims to help CASimanwa aspirants on how to get in our University or simply ask for tips as they try their luck into getting in. Most of these aspirants do not have the privilege to stay updated in online portals offered by WVSU or get insiders tips on how to get in and survive WVSU-CAS. CAScouts: Shaping future Taga-CAS is a platform that offers this solution, Scouting future taga-CAS in highschools around Iloilo especially in areas far from the city where staying updated about WVSU/CAS affairs is a continuous battle is an instrument of giving them a chance to change the course of their lives. Given that we could only conduct atleast five face to face CAScouts event we can give out the pdf copies of each organization pamphlet and the promotional videos to SSGs from schools around Iloilo that we cannot visit so that atleast despite not being there physically we can still offer them help in having a higher chance of getting in the College of Arts and Sciences.

CASugilanon

Let Your Voice Echo


A series of Public Speaking workshops directly under CASABAK sa PAGTILILINGOG open for ALL STUDENTS UNDER THE COLLEGE OF ARTS AND SCIENCES. This program will help them become a thought leader through boosting their confidence which they can use in career advancements, academic purposes, and help them get comfortable in social settings.
