Project Ulikid

A campus extension program specializing in literacy.

This project aims to adapt a community and establish a core foundation among the beneficiaries in all 5 areas of literacy namely: Reading, Writing, Numeracy, Speaking, and Listening. while simultaneously stimulating the interest and self responsibility of the WVSU students to take part in community work that tackles issues that are vital to the development of the community. Pursuing this project will allow us to open a door of opportunity for our beneficiaries to make a better lives for themselves this will also give WVSU a chance to strengthen their relationship with the community and their stakeholders and as well as create a brand for themselves as a University that is willing to foster change and impactful outcomes in our society one community at a time.
