Platform

Discover more about CICT - USA Platforms.

Rehabilitation of CICT Sheds


The Rehabilitation of CICT Sheds platform, driven by the USA-CICT Slate, is a community-led initiative aimed at revitalizing the outdated sheds in the CICT area by repainting and repairing them. Our focus is on providing convenient and comfortable spaces for CICTzens. We believe that CICTzens are the primary stakeholders in this project, and we are committed to involving the community in the process to ensure that the rehabilitated sheds meet their unique needs and preferences. Our goal is to create vibrant and welcoming spaces that enhance the quality of life for all CICTzens.

Mobilization of RFID


The Mobilization of RFID platform is an innovative solution for attendance-taking and data management for CICTzens. By using Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology, we can simplify and streamline the attendance process, making it quicker and more efficient. With this technology, attendance data can be recorded and managed in real-time, allowing for more accurate and up-to-date attendance records. Additionally, our platform can provide valuable insights into attendance patterns and trends, enabling better decision-making and resource allocation.

Leaders Cybergence


Leaders Cybergence is an innovative platform designed to help aspiring leaders in the field of information and communication technology (ICT) to develop their skills, gain knowledge, and become future leaders in the industry. Our mission is to cultivate and train CICTzens who are interested in pursuing leadership roles and who are committed to making a positive impact on society through the use of technology.

Esports Cup


The eSports Cup for CICTzens is a dynamic platform that builds on the success of the College of ICT as the eSports Capital of West Visayas State University. Our platform provides a space for CICTzens to showcase their gaming skills and compete against their peers in a range of popular eSports games.

Abaga para sa kapwa


"Abaga para sa Kapwa" is an innovative platform designed to help CICTzens who are in need, especially those who have been affected by calamities. Our outreach program aims to provide support and assistance by empowering them through income-generating projects.
