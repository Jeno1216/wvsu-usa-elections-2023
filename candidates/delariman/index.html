    CASama sa CAsiguraduhan

    Under this project are two initiatives, first is aiming to provide transparency records especially in terms of minutes of general assemblies. Furthermore, this provides a track record wherein CASimanwas can easily access the printed minutes attached in the bulletin boards. Publication materials template will be made available during the start of the first semester with the help of the creatives wherein this will be utilized as templates for gists of general assembly minutes which will be sent to the group chats of homerooms, curriculums, and organizations so to limit the miscommunication among the CASimanwas.

    Along with the aim for transparency is the aim to share the knowledge on how to handle transparency. With this, discussions will be made especially for secretaries and their counterparts as well as anyone who would like to participate. This will further aim to educate the participants on what to do during parliamentary meetings which is practiced in the college. This will be useful especially among freshmen and newbie secretaries.

    Sigurado ang Health Mo!

    As a volunteer of a medical mission team for about ten years, I have been exposed to the medical frontline. WIth this, this will aim to provide health education and free distribution of vitamins. This will be in partnership with the Department of Health, health-concerned organizations under the college, and medical mission teams interested. Furthermore, if circumstances permit, medicine sponsors and health professional partners could be utilized to further maximize the aim of the project.
