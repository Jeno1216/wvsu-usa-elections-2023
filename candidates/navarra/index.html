Platform

PROJECT TECHNOVATION

To foster the integration of technological innovation among WVSU and future Taga-Wests.

Platforms under PROJECT TECHNOVATION:

AMBOT
This is a chatbot thats aims to automatically respond to University and WVSU CAT-related questions to make information accessible for everyone anytime and anywhere. This chatbot can answer questions such as how to process a certain document in the registrar. This innovation can also cater questions regarding the WVSU CAT such as requirements for WVSU CAT Applications. We acknowledge the struggles of those who are new in the institution and aspirants of West Visayas State University especially when these information are not accessible due to some circumstances. This innovative idea aims to fill this gap in oder to make information accessible with ease and convenience for Taga-Wests and those who aspire to be.

E-Check Attendance
A lot of students find it very inconvenient to list their names during attendance whenever there will be a university or college event. E-Check Attendance aims to create an intensified attendance system for all college student councils through mobilization of student barcodes for fast and easy checking of attendance. Instead of manually writing your name in a piece of paper, the utilization of scanners to scan the student barcodes to list the attendance will help the college student councils save time and paper as we will be using innovative approach through the use of technology instead of using traditional approach to checking of attendance which takes time and hassle.

Techno Summit
This aims to establish technology-related seminar/workshops such as basic digital portfolio building or graphic designing. Although these topics is mainly focused for students in CICT, it is now the time that we open this to every taga-West students who have interest in these fields because we are now living in the 21st century and these workshops could help a lot of Taga-West develop skills that are pivotal in this digital generation where in digital literacy is a must. By having this, it could open up so many opportunities for Taga-West before and after they graduate because they could develop life-longs skills that they can use someday because technology-related skills is very in demand nowadays.
