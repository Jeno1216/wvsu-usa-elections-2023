Platform

LimpYOUng Taga-CAS


This platform drives the students to observe sanitation at all times. It is a related platform with the ongoing CAS PADtry and Project 437. It aims to (1) promote sanitation and cleanliness; (2) encourage the CAS students to practice safe and healthy hygiene, and; (3) prevent the spread of any disease, particularly COVID-19.

KaSEAmanwa sa Paglimpyo


Description
This platform aims to conduct coastal clean-ups within Iloilo. It is open for everyone to participate, as this project aims that everyone - every CAS-related course, CAS college organization officers, and curriculum officers will be represented.

Purpose
It seeks to: (1) serve as a means of connecting with nature; (2) promote environmental sustainability, and; (3) boost interaction among fellow CAS students who will join.
