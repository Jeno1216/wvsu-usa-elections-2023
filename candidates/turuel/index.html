Project SEEK

Sustainable Environment providing Efficient Knowledge for Learning in WVSU

As the face-to-face set-up of classes with the observance of the COVID-19 pandemic becomes the new normal, students are now indulged in various locations or places where they could be comfortable with their studies whenever they review for examinations, in creating their projects and activities, and even for leisure purposes. West Visayas State University was able to create and establish buildings and infrastructures to accommodate students such as libraries and parks as most of the classrooms are designed as science and computer laboratories. However, with the increasing number of students enrolling and degree programs to be added in the future, the institution needs to develop more buildings and spaces for the students since the school library is fully occupied. This has led to students settling and seeking for study hubs and/or academically inclined coffee shops although it requires payment in order to do and finish their tasks effectively and properly.

This platform will renovate and beautify the rusted and broken study sheds in the Mini Forest as well as the other parts. It will also include and add some innovations such as having an information board to be updated with the latest happenings in the university and whiteboards with markers to be used for discussions and other purposes. With this, we will be able to have a place wherein we can all study, do our school tasks, and for recreational activities.

Join me as we create an conducive learning environment para sa imo, para sa iya, kag para sa tanan!

Capacity Building on Parliamentary Procedure to Student Council Officers


To be part of a student council is about being knowledgeable on the basic work ethics. An elected person in position must not be only someone who has the abilities and capabilities to become a leader, but also have an intellect and literacy when it comes to dealing in formal needs of the institution. It is very given that a student body should hold meetings with its agenda, write letters, and create various reports. With all of these, it has seen that the elected students are not that well-educated and informed yet if how to address one another during meetings, how should write a proper and formal letters, and how must one do their reports efficiently such as accomplishment reports, liquidations, and even presentation of achievements as as student body - the need for appropriate rapport and camaderie. It is time and it is a need that leaders should be only someone who empowers and delegates, but someone that knows how to be appropriate and refined.

The platform aims to provide workshop training to be knowledgeable for appropriate parliamentary procedures. The elected student councils to learn the basic work ethics, correct ways of writing various letters, how to produce reports properly, and how to hold favorable and productive meetings. This promotes appropriateness and formality for everyone as these students may apply this ethical approach in entering the corporate world or involving in local or national politics or governance in the future. Everyone will not be just an outrageous and purposeful leader, but strong, intelligent, and literate leaders.
