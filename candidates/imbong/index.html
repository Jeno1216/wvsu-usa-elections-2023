CASafe Space

Pamatin-an ang CASakit

This project is determined to create a safe space for CASimanwas where they are free to share their vulnerabilities, worries, concerns, and problems. This can be accomplished by creating forms where they can freely vent out and come as they are. The confessions will be posted creatively with anonymity which was inspired by Geloy Concepcion’s concept. CASubscribe sa CASpotify playlist will also be posted and shared to CASimanwas every first day of the month. This is to let them feel that they are not alone as they enter into a new month - a fresh start. This project can provide support, and comfort and can serve as an avenue for students to feel that they are not alone in the struggles that they are facing. Above all, it promotes a healthy way of coping and handling emotions.

Proyekto CASalig



Disseminating information to the student body is vital. It plays a huge role in bridging oneness and unity of one’s college. Delivering relevant, quality, credible, and reliable information is what the students deserve to have. And that is the main objective of this advocacy – Proyekto CASalig. This is accomplished by sending actively and consistently the minutes of the meeting to the student body, updating diligently and punctually the information board, knowledgeable about the information disseminated, and above all, ensuring the reliability, credibility, and relevance of the information before it is shared.
